Our Journey

Our story

ICSM Midstream and Trading Africa (Pty) Ltd was founded in 2011 with a vision to become a leading fuel and specialty chemicals distribution and trading company in South Africa. Since our inception, we have worked tirelessly to build a reputation for excellence, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

About Us

What we stand for

  • Our Mission

    Our mission is to provide our customers with high-quality fuel and specialty chemicals products, delivered safely and efficiently. We strive to build long-term relationships with our customers, suppliers, and partners, and to contribute to the growth and development of the energy sector in South Africa.

  • Our Values

    At ICSM Midstream and Trading Africa, we are guided by a set of core values that shape our culture and behavior. These values include:

    • • Safety: We prioritize the safety of our employees, customers, and the environment.
    • • Integrity: We operate with transparency, honesty, and ethics in all our dealings.
    • • Customer Focus: We are committed to delivering exceptional customer service and support.
    • • Teamwork: We believe in collaboration, mutual respect, and open communication.
    • • Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do.

  • Our Team

    Our team is comprised of experienced professionals with a deep understanding of the energy sector. Our employees are dedicated, passionate, and committed to delivering exceptional results.
